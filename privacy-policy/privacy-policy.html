We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our App and store certain information. Tracking technologies used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our App. The technologies we use may include:

Certain sections of our App and our emails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit the Company, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for other related app statistics (for example, recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).
